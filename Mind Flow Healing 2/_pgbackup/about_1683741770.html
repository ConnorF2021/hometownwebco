About Me

Hi, my name is Alexis Colasurdo. I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree for Mechanical Engineering at Penn State. Through my journey to where I am now, I have encountered many challenges that brought me to PSYCH-K®. For over a year, after going through mental and spiritual challenges, I spent my time looking for the problems and pondering on the negatives. Like any normal teenager, I felt as though my life was over the second their was a bump in the road. I had my dream career shatter in front of me and unfortunately spent a lot of time in a dark place. PSYCH-K® reminded me of the possibilities and opportunities ahead and how to capture and live my own path, helping others to unlock their potential and live their life, simply and happily. After going through a PSYCH-K Basic Workshop and Master Facilitation Worshop, I am exstatic about PSYCH-K® and sharing this gift with everyone who is willing to listen. I now use PSYCH-K® Balances to improve my daily life and I cannot wait to work with those individuals willing to take charge of their own life as well.
